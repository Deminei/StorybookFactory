Storybook Factory



How To Get Started:

Step 1

Embark on your storytelling journey by immersing yourself in the provided prompt. Allow it to serve as your compass, guiding you in the selection of your imaginative pathway.

Step 2

Discern the most enthralling route that propels your story onward. Engage with the "Continue" button or opt for "Enter" to enrich the narrative gradually. As you weave your tale, each click contributes to the mosaic of your captivating adventure. When you feel the tale is complete, embrace the finale by selecting the resolute red "End" button. The opportunity now presents itself to "Generate Storybook."

Step 3

This selection breathes life into your narrative, sculpting it into a visual masterpiece, complete with complementary illustrations. Traverse the entirety of your creation on the website, and upon reaching the culmination, trigger transformation with the click of the "Export" button. Witness your narrative metamorphose into a PDF.

Step 4

Upon your selection of the "Export" button, the portal to the PDF realm opens. Within its digital embrace, the opportunity emerges to transcribe your story into reality, page by page, with the simple act of pressing "Print."
